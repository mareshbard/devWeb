How To Play

1- The game will randomly select a secret number within a predefined range.
2- You need to guess the secret number.
3-Enter your guess into the provided input field and submit.
4- After each guess, you will receive feedback on whether your guess is too high, too low, or correct.
5- Use the feedback to refine your next guess.
6= Keep guessing until you correctly identify the secret number or reach the maximum number of allowed attempts.
